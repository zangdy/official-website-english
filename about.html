Company profile

Anhui ruimeng Technology Co., Ltd

Anhui ruimeng Technology Co., Ltd. is located in the development zone of Mengcheng County, Anhui Province, China. It is a building energy-saving high-tech enterprise integrating the R & D, production and sales of thermal insulation materials and building thermal insulation materials. The project covers a total area of 223347 square meters, with a total investment of US $157 million. There are two automatic production lines with an annual output of 10 square meters in phase I. Two automatic production lines with an annual output of 1.4 million square meters in phase II. The total construction area is about 140000 square meters, including plant, office building, dormitory building, R & D building, etc.

The company has a new intelligent production line of inorganic thermal insulation decorative board with an annual output of 5 million square meters and relevant supporting facilities. It is mainly engaged in the production, R & D and sales of industrial thermal insulation products and ceramic products, the R & D and sales of exterior wall thermal insulation panels, and the R & D and sales of building materials.

Raymond inorganic thermal insulation decorative board not only has a series of superior properties such as thermal insulation, sound absorption and noise reduction, waterproof and moisture-proof, fire prevention, acid and alkali resistance, but also has the inherent advantages of permanence, safety, reliability, chemical corrosion resistance and protection from ants and rats. It integrates decorative effect and thermal insulation function. Therefore, the product can effectively solve the construction problems in the construction industry, accelerate the application of advanced technology in the new wall material industry, and play a leading role in effectively solving key technical problems and the development of thermal insulation and energy saving industry.

Institute of inorganic materials

Founded in 2008, inorganic materials research institute is a scientific research institution that relies on the R & D and production base of Fanghao industry and ruimeng Technology Co., Ltd. to carry out technological innovation and system integration of non-metallic mineral energy-saving materials. It has a public technical service platform for inorganic building energy-saving materials, a national key laboratory, a provincial key laboratory, a perlite processing and application engineering laboratory Perlite products engineering technology research center, enterprise technology center and other R & D platforms. Our institute has absorbed many top R & D experts and talents in China, established a joint development mechanism with many domestic well-known universities and institutions, achieved more than 20 scientific research achievements every year, and successfully presided over a number of national, provincial and ministerial R & D topics and scientific and technological projects.
