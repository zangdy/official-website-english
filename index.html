公司介绍

Anhui ruimeng Technology Co., Ltd., located in the development zone of Mengcheng County, Anhui Province, is a building energy-saving high-tech enterprise integrating the R & D, production and sales of industrial thermal insulation materials and building thermal insulation materials. The project covers a total area of 223347 square meters, with a total investment of US $157 million. There are two automatic production lines with an annual output of 110 square meters in phase I. Two automatic production lines with an annual output of 1.4 million square meters in phase II. The total construction area is about 140000 square meters.

Raymond inorganic thermal insulation decorative board not only has a series of superior properties such as thermal insulation, sound absorption and noise reduction, waterproof and moisture-proof, fire prevention, acid and alkali resistance, but also has the inherent advantages of permanence, safety, reliability, chemical corrosion resistance and protection from ants and rats. It integrates decorative effect and thermal insulation function.
